# Jeju Island Itinerary (Jul 18–20)

Group of 4, arriving Jul 18 from Fukuoka and departing Jul 20 to Busan, both via
Jeju's airport (in/out of Jeju City side).

## Base decision
Considered Jeju City, Seogwipo/Jungmun, west coast (Aewol/Hallim), and east coast
(Seongsan) as bases. Decided: **rent a car, base in Jeju City both nights**, and
day-trip out. Reasoning — cheap per-head with 4 people splitting one car, English
road signs and decent highways make self-driving easy (Korean license not
required with an International Driving Permit), and a car turns "pick one base"
into a genuinely flexible two-day loop without needing to relocate overnight.

## Day 1 — Jeju City & north coast
- Iho Tewoo Beach
- Yongduam Rock
- Dongmun Traditional Market
- Hamdeok Beach *(flagged: can get busy by midday in July)*
- Sarabong Park (sunset)
- Black pork BBQ dinner near the city

Stop order within Day 1 is flexible — all clustered near Jeju City.

## Day 2 — South coast loop
Drive south via the cross-island road:
- Cheonjiyeon Waterfall
- Jeongbang Waterfall
- Seogwipo Maeil Olle Market (lunch)
- Oedolgae Rock
- Jusangjeolli Cliff
- Jungmun Saekdal Beach
- Drive back to Jeju City

## Options considered but not used
An earlier draft paired the south coast day with an **east coast day** instead
(Seongsan Ilchulbong sunrise hike + Udo Island + Manjanggul lava tube +
Woljeongri Beach) — discarded in favor of the north-coast/Jeju-City day above
per explicit request. Worth resurfacing if the group wants alternatives for a
future trip or has spare time.

## Reminder
Jeju is visa-free for entry, but does **not** permit onward mainland Korea
travel for the two travelers with mainland visa constraints — this is why the
Jeju→Busan leg only has Aleks and Ralph confirmed (see `korea-visa-constraint.md`
and `seoul-airport-logistics.md`).
