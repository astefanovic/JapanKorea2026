# Seoul → Incheon Endgame (Jul 21–23)

Applies to Aleks + Ralph specifically (the two travelers confirmed on this leg).

## The problem
Final flight is Cebu Pacific 5J185, ICN → MNL → MEL, departing **06:35 on Jul 23**.
Public transport can't reliably get you to ICN in time for a 6:30am departure:
- AREX first train from Seoul Station doesn't run until ~06:10am (Incheon T2 first
  train ~05:15am) — too late.
- Airport limousine buses start ~4am at earliest, journey 50min–1h45 depending on
  route/traffic — too tight against a ~2.5–3hr pre-departure buffer.
- Night buses (N6000/N6001/N6002) are infrequent and not practical with luggage.
- **Conclusion**: a taxi/Kakao T is the only mode with reliable margin for an
  early-hours departure (~₩70–80k from central Seoul + ₩5.5–6.6k toll + 20% night
  surcharge 00:00–04:00).

## The chosen plan (avoids the early-morning problem entirely)
Rather than fight pre-dawn transit, arrive at the airport the evening before via
normal-hours transport and overnight there:

1. **Jul 21, night**: KTX 072 Busan → Seoul, dep ~22:30, arr ~00:27 (seats 12C/12D).
   Chosen over flying Busan→Gimpo: shorter door-to-door (~2.5hrs vs ~3.5–4hrs) and
   fewer transfer points (flying would still need a Gimpo→Incheon transfer after landing).
2. **Late check-in** at ZIYOLK Seoul Station guesthouse (self-check-in kiosk —
   needed since arrival is ~00:30).
3. **Jul 22**: full day in Seoul (no second hotel night needed).
4. **Jul 22, night**: last AREX Express, 23:00 from Seoul Station → Incheon, while
   AREX is still running normal hours.
5. **Overnight at ICN** before the 06:35 departure on the 23rd. Options identified:
   Darakhyu capsule hotel, or Spa on Air / Spa at Home jjimjilbang.
6. Stay at ibis Styles Ambassador Incheon T2 (Jul 22–23) — confirmed booking exists
   for this, may supersede the airport-overnight-only plan above; check Drive for
   which is actually booked.

## Other logistics settled
- **KTX booking**: KorailTalk app recommended over the Korail website — foreign
  cards work better on the app. Tickets open 30 days before travel.
- **Luggage storage**: T-Luggage's staffed counter at Seoul Station, with airport
  delivery (~₩20,000), preferred over coin lockers due to peak-season availability risk.
