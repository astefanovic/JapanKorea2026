## Logistics Notes

- **Shinkansen intercity:** Book individual tickets (Osaka, Fukuoka legs). Luggage over 160cm total dimensions needs oversized baggage seat reservation — or use Takuhaibin (hotel-to-hotel forwarding).
- **Universal Studios:** Download official USJ app to secure Area Timed Entry Ticket for Super Nintendo World.
- **Incheon airport:** ibis Styles is at T2 — return flight departs T1. Free shuttle ~18 min, starts ~05:00.
